Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

The Shadow Grid ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Joker Spades mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, your choices, and how we protect your data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. These are stored securely on servers hosted by Amazon Web Services (AWS). A unique user identifier is generated automatically when you create an account.

1.2 Sign in with Apple

If you sign in with Apple, we receive the information you authorize Apple to share, which typically includes your name and email address. We use this solely to create and authenticate your account. We do not access any other Apple account data.

1.3 Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we request access to your basic profile information (name and email address) via the email and profile OAuth scopes. We use this information solely to create and authenticate your account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, drive, or any other Google services. Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1.4 Game Data

We store your game statistics locally on your device, including wins, losses, rank progression, tricks won, bids, and streaks. If you create an account, your rank and basic win/loss record may be synced to our servers to enable online multiplayer features, tournaments, and leaderboards.

1.5 Social and Friend Data

If you use the friends feature, we store:

  • Your friend list and friend requests (sent and received)
  • Your friend tag (a display name plus a 4-digit code, e.g., "Player#1234")
  • Your online/offline status, visible to your accepted friends

This data is stored on our servers to enable friend search, game invitations, and online play.

1.6 Tournament and Leaderboard Data

If you participate in tournaments, your display name, game variant, and win count are recorded on our servers and may appear on public leaderboards visible to other players.

1.7 Token Storage

Authentication tokens are stored using platform-native encrypted storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences). Tokens are never stored in plaintext. ID tokens expire after 1 hour and refresh tokens expire after 30 days.

1.8 Subscription Data

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive confirmation of your subscription status from RevenueCat, our subscription management provider, which communicates with the App Store or Play Store on our behalf. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

1.9 Advertising Data

The free version of the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Google Advertising ID on Android)
  • General device information (device model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data may be used to serve personalized or contextual advertisements. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings or by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS. Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements and no advertising data is collected for them. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

1.10 Guest Mode

You can use the App without creating an account. In guest mode, a random identifier is generated locally on your device to maintain your local game progress. This identifier is not sent to our servers unless you later create an account, in which case it is used to link your local progress to your new account.

1.11 Automatically Collected Information

Other than information collected by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) and subscription partner (RevenueCat) as described above, we do not use additional third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not independently collect device identifiers, location data, or browsing history.

1.12 Preferences and Settings

Your visual and gameplay preferences (theme, card style, volume levels, favorite emotes, etc.) are stored locally on your device only. These preferences are not uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App and its core gameplay features
  • To authenticate your account for online features
  • To enable multiplayer gameplay, friend connections, and game invitations
  • To display tournament leaderboards
  • To sync your game rank across devices (if you create an account)
  • To verify your subscription status and provide premium features
  • To display advertisements to non-subscriber users
  • To improve the App and diagnose technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Encryption in transit: All API communication uses HTTPS and WSS (encrypted WebSocket) protocols.
  • Encryption at rest: Server-side encryption is enabled on all database tables (AWS DynamoDB with SSE).
  • Secure token storage: Authentication tokens are stored using iOS Keychain and Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • Authenticated access: All server requests require a valid authentication token. WebSocket connections are authenticated at connection time.
  • Input validation: User inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent injection attacks.
  • Network security: The Android build blocks cleartext (unencrypted) HTTP traffic.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Authentication tokens: ID tokens expire after 1 hour; refresh tokens expire after 30 days.
  • WebSocket connections: Automatically cleaned up via time-to-live expiration.
  • Lobby and matchmaking data: Automatically deleted within 30 minutes of creation.
  • Tournament entries: Retained for the duration of the tournament season.
  • Account deletion: If you delete your account, all associated data (profile, game statistics, friend relationships, tournament entries) is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Local data on your device is cleared immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the App. Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will remove your profile, game data, friends list, tournament entries, and authentication credentials from our servers.
  • Local Data: You can clear all local data by uninstalling the App.
  • Guest Mode: You can use the App without creating an account. In guest mode, all data remains on your device only.
  • Ad Personalization: On iOS, you can decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt to prevent personalized ads. On Android, you can opt out of ad personalization in your device's Google Settings. You can also subscribe to Premium to remove all ads entirely.
  • Revoking Sign-In Access: You can revoke Google Sign-In access at any time through your Google Account permissions. You can revoke Apple Sign-In access through your Apple ID settings under Settings > [Your Name] > Sign-In & Security > Sign in with Apple.
  • Friend Visibility: You can remove friends at any time through the Friends screen. Removing a friend will remove your visibility to them and theirs to you.

8. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States (AWS US-East-1 region). If you access the App from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below or use the in-app account deletion feature.
